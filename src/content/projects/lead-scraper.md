A practical lead-generation pipeline for local businesses: it scrapes multiple
sources, enriches each lead with verified contact details, phone lookups, and
tech-stack detection, scores them, and exports clean spreadsheets for outreach. It's
the data engine that feeds Flash Outreach, my AI cold-email system.
