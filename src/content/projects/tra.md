TRA is a B2B SaaS for travel requests, approvals, and reimbursements — the modern,
multi-tenant rebuild of the Power Platform system I architected at Mobile County
Public Schools for 92 schools and 7,200 staff.

## Why it's credible

I'm not guessing at this domain — I lived it. The original handled estimate
submission, GL processing, audit tracking, multi-role review, and HTML-to-PDF
archival in production for three years. TRA carries that hard-won workflow knowledge
onto a current stack:

- **Next.js 16 / React 19** app router with admin, approvals, dashboard, requests,
  reimbursements, and settings routes.
- **Prisma + PostgreSQL** data layer.
- **WorkOS AuthKit** for enterprise SSO.

It's the bridge between my enterprise track record and where I'm taking it.
